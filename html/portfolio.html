CYPSA

Project is centered on optimizing threat detection and monitoring through intuitive graphical representations of network activities. This initiative seeks to streamline risk assessment and compliance efforts by presenting vulnerabilities and potential threats in a more accessible manner. Leveraging the RESLab Testbed and CyPRES EMS, the project aims to integrate FMECA and a Risk Matrix, enhancing its capability to spot flaws in machinery and systems and prioritize cybersecurity risks. The ongoing work aims to create a holistic solution that combines data acquisition, cyber attack simulation, data fusion algorithms, and a user-friendly CyPSA interface for improved cybersecurity measures.
